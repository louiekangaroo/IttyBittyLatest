BECOME AN EXHIBITOR

Intro

As an exclusive parenting and children’s event, Itty Bitty Kids Expo is a fantastic way for exhibitors to gain extensive exposure of their products or services, increase sales and create strong brand awareness. With a range of affordable stand options available, packages can be adjusted to suit any budget or space requirement. Marketing support is also offered for all package options to ensure widespread and comprehensive coverage.

Stand Options Available

Shell Scheme: walling, carpet, power, lighting, fascia board (with company name)

Space Only: power, lighting and carpeted floor space only

For further information about becoming an exhibitor and securing a space at the expo, please complete the ‘Register your Interest’ form and we will be in contact with you shortly.

BECOME A SPONSOR

Event sponsorship is an ideal opportunity to promote your brand and to advertise your products and services leading up to, during and post the 2015 Itty Bitty Kids Expo. Exclusive sponsorship packages are now available.

More than just putting your brand centre stage at this major event, we provide value to our sponsors with multi-faceted media packages that deliver your key branding statements across multiple platforms. Each sponsorship package is designed to be used as a creative marketing tool to showcase your products and services to our valued patrons attending the event.

If you would like more information about becoming a sponsor, please complete the ‘Register your Interest’ form and we will be in contact with you shortly

BECOME A VOLUNTEER

Volunteering at the 2015 Itty Bitty Kids Expo is a great way to get involved and have fun while making a positive contribution to the local community. There are many opportunities leading up to the event and on the day to help make this year’s expo a success.

If you are interested in volunteering, registrations open in August 2015.
